Knowledge Center

üßº 5 Simple Tips for Clean Selling

  1. Wash hands frequently: Use soap and clean water before handling vegetables
  2. Cover produce: Protect vegetables from dust, flies, and contamination
  3. Use clean surfaces: Keep selling areas and cutting boards sanitized
  4. Separate raw items: Avoid cross-contamination between different produce
  5. Regular cleaning: Clean stalls and equipment daily with disinfectant

üëê Proper Handwashing Guide

  1. Wet hands with clean, running water
  2. Apply soap and lather well
  3. Scrub for at least 20 seconds
  4. Clean under nails and between fingers
  5. Rinse thoroughly with clean water
  6. Dry with clean towel or air dry

When to wash: Before handling food, after using restroom, after touching surfaces, after coughing or sneezing

ü•¨ Vegetable Storage Best Practices

  • Temperature control: Keep vegetables in cool, shaded areas
  • Proper ventilation: Ensure good air circulation
  • Avoid ground contact: Use clean mats or raised platforms
  • Regular inspection: Remove spoiled items immediately
  • Clean containers: Use sanitized baskets and bags
